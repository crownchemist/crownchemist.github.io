The Artist

Hi, I’m Katie G ✨

I’m a Rogue Valley native and lifelong artist whose creativity has taken shape through many mediums, including digital art and graphic design. My love for fashion, style, and self-expression started early — I was doing my own hair as a teenager — and it naturally grew into a career behind the chair.

I love helping clients discover looks that feel authentically theirs, whether that’s something bold and expressive or effortlessly wearable. I’m very detail-oriented (a bit of a perfectionist!), and I’m always pursuing ongoing education so I can bring fresh techniques and thoughtful artistry to every appointment. I specialize in wide-range of colors, including vivids, along with blonding, and custom cuts. My experience spans from expressive to everyday wear alike.

As a local, my goal is to bring more variety and creativity to our community while prioritizing healthy, sustainable hair. I’m passionate about helping you achieve your dream look without compromising the integrity of your hair — all in a welcoming, fun environment.

I can’t wait to have you in my chair
